Holistic User Models for Food Recommendation

Hello,

In this experiment you will receive some recommendations about FOOD you can enjoy.
These recommendations are based on your personal characteristics as well as on the popularity of the recipe.
In the following, we will ask you some information about you, your food preferences and food habits.
Next, syou will receive two different food recommendations and you will be asked to choose the one you prefer.
Finally, you have to fill in a short questionnaire evaluating to what extent each recipe can help you reaching your goals.
The whole experiment will take less than five minutes.
